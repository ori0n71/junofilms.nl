About:
Juno.

Hiya, My name's Juno, a 23 year old camera and video enthusiast. This portfolio was formed to share the work I love creating.
The interesting people I cross pathes with spark my urge to create visuals. My knowledge and skill in lighting and audiovisual production are only a vessel, through which I highlight the very people I find worth remembering.
Lately I have found myself zoning in on filming creative interview setups, where I get to play with lighting and color-grading as much as possible.
I have yet to figure out what the goal is. What grand project I want to have define me, or what is to eventually bring me fulfillment. Until then, I plan to enjoy the road.
